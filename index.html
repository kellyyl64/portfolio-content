This is a project for SI539. The project targets to examine how different video games depict cultures. Cultures depicted may be from the production team own background and also may differ from the production team background.

The selected games include: Genshin Impact, Persona 5 Royal, and Assassin Creed Origin. Cultures presented are: Genshin Impact - China, Persona 5 Royal - modern day Japan, Assassin Creed Origin - ancient Egypt.
